Bio

I'm currently a graduate student at Columbia University studying Computer Science.

At Barnard College, I earned my BA in Computer Science and Psychology. Exploring these fields led me to develop a love for interdisciplinary, creative computing. Upon graduating, I assumed a position as a Post-Baccalaureate Fellow at Barnard's Vagelos Computational Science Center (CSC). This role allowed me to engage in game development research and support various student programming. I was able to realize multiple independent projects in game design and data science, simultaneously advancing my pedagogical skills.

Now, I continue to seek out ways to bridge technology, design, human cognition, and education.
